# projects

A collection of hackathon wins and open-source contributions.

### [Summa](https://github.com/MontaQLabs/summa)
SHE (Somewhat Homomorphic Encryption) library on Polkadot PVM. Features Twisted ElGamal, Bulletproofs range proofs, DLEQ proofs, and UTXO shielded notes. Built with Rust.

### [SubLua](https://github.com/PolkadotOpenSourceGrants/apply/pull/24)
Lightweight Substrate SDK for building onchain games using Lua. Pure C/Lua. Funded by the **Polkadot Open-Source Bounty**.

### [Relay](http://www.relayapp.org)
Web3 payments ecosystem for Polkadot. Won **1st prize (\$40k)** at Polkadot Bangkok Hackathon. Recipient of a \$30k Web3 Foundation grant.

### [PRIVØRA](https://github.com/abhiraj-mengade)
Privacy investment platform. **ZypherPunk Hackathon** winner, Near AI track (\$5k). Built with Near AI and Rust.

### [Flow](https://www.moveathon.build/projectgallery#apaceurope)
Top 3 at **Moveathon Global: Europe Edition '25**. Built with Next.js and Move.

### [GreenTrust](https://github.com/abhiraj-mengade/GreenTrust)
Organic farming certification platform. Top 3 at **Polkadot Global Europe '23**, Paris. Built with Next.js, Rust, and Polkadot.

### [Chargeswap](https://devfolio.co/projects/chargeswap-3527)
EV battery swapping platform. **ETH-India '22** winner; recipient of a \$5k Filecoin grant. Built with Solidity and IoT.

### [MontaQ Labs](https://montaq.org)
Web3 protocols & infra startup. Backed by the Web3 Foundation. Built with Rust and IoT.
