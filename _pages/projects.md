# projects

A collection of hackathon wins and open-source contributions.

### [ZKAMA](https://github.com/jatinsahijwani/zk-polka-sdk)
Zero-Knowledge Verifier SDK for Kusama PolkaVM. Won **1st prize** at Polkadot AssetHub Hackathon in Goa. Reduces complex ZK workflows into simple CLI commands and TypeScript calls.

### [Relay](http://www.relayapp.org)
Payment ecosystem app for Polkadot. Won **1st prize** at Polkadot Hackathon: Bangkok Edition. Recipient of a W3F Grant.

### [Summa](https://github.com/MontaQLabs/summa)
SHE (Somewhat Homomorphic Encryption) library on Polkadot PVM. Features Twisted ElGamal, Bulletproofs range proofs, and DLEQ proofs. Built with Rust.

### [SubLua](https://github.com/PolkadotOpenSourceGrants/apply/pull/24)
Lightweight Substrate SDK for building onchain games using Lua. Funded by Polkadot Open-Source Bounty.

### [PolkaArena](https://polkarena.montaq.org/)
Hackathon management platform for all Polkadot hackathons. Currently in development with an MVP available.

### [Khoj](https://github.com/Polkadot-Fast-Grants/apply/blob/master/applications/khoj.md#team-members)
Treasure hunt dapp on Polkadot. Winner at **ETHIndia 2024**. Recipient of a W3F Fast Grant.

### [ChargeSwap](https://devfolio.co/projects/chargeswap-3527)
EV battery swapping platform built with Solidity and IoT. Winner at **ETHIndia 2022**. Recipient of a $5k Filecoin grant.

### [Firstblock](https://firstblock.montaq.org/)
Education platform for newcomers in the Polkadot ecosystem.
